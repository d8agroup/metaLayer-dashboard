"Using deLv is easy! To get started..."

Step 1

Begin by drag a Data Point on to the canvas, configure it, and click save.

Step 2

Apply an action by dragging it on to the Data Point you've just collected. Action is simply our word for algorithms which transform your content in ways that are useful.

Step 3

DeLv allows you come to quick conclusions or make discoveries from messy data sets like social media streams, news articles, or feeds from the web. We do all the hard work so that for you it's all drag and drop!
