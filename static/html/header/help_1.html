"using metaLayer is as easy as 1, 2, 3 ..."

Step 1

Start by dragging one of these data points into one of the 'Drag and Drop Data' areas, configure it and click save

Step 2

Apply one of our custom built actions by dragging it on to the content you just collected.

Step 3

And now its really up to you, you can curate these lists of content by filtering by the actions you have applied and then, if you want to, you can choose to either visualize or output the results by dragging down a visualization or an output type!
